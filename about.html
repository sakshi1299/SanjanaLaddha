Who am I?

I am a 21-year old multi-disciplinary designer. I specialize in crafting exciting digital and brand experiences through research, design and technology.

What design means to me?

Design to me is a way of thinking, which when put to use has the potential to shape the world. I believe in strong, minimalist and functional design.

What do I do when I am not designing?

I love to read fiction, write poems, code creative concepts, dance in public and photograph still life objects.
